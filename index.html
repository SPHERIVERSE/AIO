Configurations & Models for Professional Use

Specifications Requirements

  • Processor: Intel i5 12th Gen or above (e.g., 13420H)
  • Display: 99-100% sRGB, FHD IPS
  • Brightness: >250 nits
  • RAM: 8GB / 16GB (preferable)
  • Storage: 512GB DDR5 SSD (expandable)
  • OS: Windows 10/11 (latest)
  • Graphics: Integrated sufficient, optionally dedicated for high-end graphics
  • Laptop Backup Time: >5 hours
  • Ports: At least 2x USB-A, 1x USB-C, Ethernet, HDMI
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 5/6
  • Power Backup: UPS mandatory
